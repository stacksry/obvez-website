About Obvez Labs

A disciplined partner for pharmaceutical testing.

Headquartered in Hyderabad, India, Obvez Labs Pvt. Ltd. is a contract research organization serving pharmaceutical manufacturers across domestic and regulated international markets. Our facility is equipped with advanced analytical instrumentation — ICP-MS, HPLC, GC head-space, ion chromatography, and FT-IR — to support complex biopharmaceuticals, nanoparticle-based formulations, and conventional dosage forms with equal rigor.

Regulatory compliance, data integrity, and patient safety are non-negotiable — in that order, on every study.

Services

Testing infrastructure, end to end.

From analytical method development through regulatory submission support — one accountable partner for every phase of your testing program.

01 — Analytical

Analytical services.

High-precision chemical analysis using chromatography, spectroscopy, and wet-chemistry techniques.

  • Method development & validation (ICH Q2R)
  • Method verification & transfer (USP <1224>, <1226>)
  • Packaging material testing (USP <661>)
  • Extractable & leachable studies
  • Metal impurities (USP <233>) & residue validation
  • Bioassays, photostability & stability studies

02 — Microbiology

Microbiology services.

Sterility, endotoxin, microbial limits, and environmental monitoring — methods matched to your dosage form.

  • Microbial limits testing (MLT), sterility & BET
  • Environmental monitoring & cleaning validations
  • Disinfectant validation & AET/PET studies
  • Water validations (Phase I, II & III)
  • MIC study & LBPC
  • Method development & validation for MLT / BET / sterility

03 — Quality

Quality management.

GMP compliance, data integrity, and regulatory readiness — engineered into every SOP and audit trail.

  • Good Documentation Practices (GDP)
  • GMP support & compliance advisory
  • Data integrity assurance & regulatory auditing
  • Risk-based quality assurance
  • Regulatory submission support for global markets
