About Obvez

A trusted partner in pharmaceutical testing.

Headquartered in Hyderabad, India, Obvez Labs Pvt. Ltd. is a client-centric analytical CRO serving pharmaceutical companies worldwide. Our facility is equipped with advanced instrumentation — from ICP-MS and HPLC to Ion Chromatography — enabling us to handle complex biopharmaceuticals, nanoparticle-based formulations, and standard dosage forms with equal proficiency.

We prioritize regulatory compliance, data integrity, and patient safety — in that order, always.

What we do

Comprehensive testing, end to end.

From method development through regulatory submission support — a single, accountable partner for every phase of your analytical program.

01 — Analytical

Analytical services.

High-precision chemical analysis using chromatography, spectroscopy, and wet-chemistry techniques.

  • Method development & validation (ICH Q2R)
  • Method verification & transfer (USP <1224>, <1226>)
  • Packaging material testing (USP <661>)
  • Extractable & leachable studies
  • Metal impurities (USP <233>) & residue validation
  • Bioassays, photostability & stability studies

02 — Microbiology

Microbiology services.

Sterility, endotoxin, microbial limits, and environmental monitoring — methods matched to your dosage form.

  • Microbial limits testing (MLT), sterility & BET
  • Environmental monitoring & cleaning validations
  • Disinfectant validation & AET/PET studies
  • Water validations (Phase I, II & III)
  • MIC study & LBPC
  • Method development & validation for MLT / BET / sterility

03 — Quality

Quality management.

GMP compliance, data integrity, and regulatory readiness — baked into every SOP.

  • Good Documentation Practices (GDP)
  • GMP support & compliance advisory
  • Data integrity assurance & regulatory auditing
  • Risk-based quality assurance
  • Regulatory submission support for global markets

04 — Development

Pharma development & QA.

Formulations, APIs, intermediates, excipients — from bench to qualification.

  • Formulation & API development
  • IQ / OQ / PQ instrument qualification
  • Vendor qualification & internal audits
  • Schedule L compliance & document archival
  • Staff qualification & training programs
